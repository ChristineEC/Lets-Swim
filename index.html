Accommodating special needs and special requests

Individual, semi-private, and small-group lessons. Beginner, intermediate and advanced. Ages 1 to 99

Lessons

Because we're all special: We offer a safe space for people from all walks of life to enjoy the wonderful world of aquatics.

Our lessons are for people of any age or ability. We set up each lesson in consultation with you and adapt the conditions to meet your needs.

Contact us directly or, better yet, use our contact form to tell us a little about your needs.

A few of the accommodations we offer:
  • Quiet hour: for students who need calm and quiet in order to participate
  • Total privacy: for people who want to stay covered up (burkinis welcome) or out of the public eye
  • Bring your own teacher or aide: for students who may benefit (special pricing available)
  • Discounts for siblings
  • Discounts for low-income families
  • Assisted pool entry and exit
  • Warm water temperature and a high pool cover for chilly days
